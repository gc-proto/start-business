3. How to apply

A complete online application package consists of a completed Project Application Form and the following attachments:

  • Signature of Applicant Authorized Representative (required)
  • Consent to Disclose Personal and/or Business Information Form (required)
  • Official Language Minority Community Assessment (voluntary)

Applicants in the Province of Québec may be required to complete an additional form to comply with the Act Respecting the Ministère du Conseil Exécutif (M-30). For more information, contact the Quebec regional office.

All projects must comply with the Canadian Environmental Assessment Act, 2012 (CEAA 2012).

How long it will take to process your online application

We may contact you for additional information. Once you submit an application we will:

  1. send an acknowledgement email to you within 1 business day;
  2. review and assess your application against the parameters and criteria of the program; and
  3. notify you in writing of final approval or rejection within 100 business days.

There are no delays expected. Online applications should be processed within 100 business days.

Download all forms in a ZIP file

Having technical problems?


In order to use the online form, you will need Adobe Reader 11.0 or above. If you don't have it, please download Adobe Reader now.

Help with alternative formats (ZIP and PDF files)

We strongly encourage you to apply online, simplifying the application process and making it faster and easier for your information to reach us.

You may wish to prepare your answers using a word processing program because many questions require lengthy responses. You can save the application form electronically and submit it from its saved location.

If you are having technical problems, please contact 1-877-246-4682 and provide the following information:

  • first and last name
  • phone number
  • description of the problem or error message of the page where you are experiencing the problem
  • computer operating system and version
  • Adobe Reader version
  • Internet browser and version
  • as much information as possible about the steps you took before you experienced the problem

Other ways to apply

You can apply by mail, email or fax by submitting the completed Application Form and required attachments to the regional office closest to you.

Contact your regional office to obtain a hard-copy application or for more information.
