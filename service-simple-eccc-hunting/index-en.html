Migratory Game Bird Hunting Permit: How to purchase a permit

From Environment and Climate Change Canada

  • How to purchase a permit
  • Contact us for help
  • More information

How to purchase a permit

To hunt migratory game birds in Canada, you are required to possess a valid federal Migratory Game Bird Hunting Permit (or e-Permit) with an affixed (or printed image of a) Canadian Wildlife Habitat Conservation stamp. Please note that you must verify the provincial/territorial regulations pertaining to hunting and the carrying of firearms for the province/territory where you will be hunting migratory game birds. Hunters who also wish to receive a physical copy of the stamp by mail can do so by checking a box when purchasing their e-Permit. There is no additional charge for shipping the stamp. The physical stamp is not required to validate the e-Permit.

How long it will take to receive your permit

Migratory Game Bird Hunting Permits can be purchased online, 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.

A printable electronic copy of the permit and the related Canadian Wildlife Habitat Conservation stamp will be issued immediately once you have completed the purchase.

Purchase a permit now

Other ways to purchase a permit

In person

There are no delays expected. Migratory Game Bird Hunting Permits are available for sale at Canada Post outlets and select independent vendors, during normal business hours.

If you have any questions with regards to the availability of the Migratory Game Bird Hunting Permits at a postal outlet, you can contact Canada Post customer service at 1-866-607-6301.
