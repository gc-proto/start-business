Information architecture, templates and detailed specifications

From Treasury Board Secretariat

The content and information architecture page templates presented in this section can be identified as either a navigation or a destination page and grouped under one of four broad categories: themes, Canada.ca collections, institutional or site-wide constructs.

As an overview, the following charts group the page templates by category, map the templates to a page type, and describe the purpose of each, when it must be used, and whether it is mandatory for Canada.ca.

To be compliant, mandatory templates must be used as defined below. Conditional and optional templates are recommended when appropriate.

Page Purpose Usage Mandate Type YYYY-MM-DD
updated
Splash Page The initial landing page for a visitor to Canada.ca, allowing the option to enter the site in French or English. Must be implemented only once on Canada.ca. Exceptions may apply* Mandatory Navigation 2017-01-01
Home Page Shows the Government of Canada’s most visited and important products and services. Must be implemented only once on Canada.ca. Mandatory Navigation 2017-01-02
Theme Pages Organizes topics that makes sense to people so they can access government-wide information and services. Must be implemented only once for each Canada.ca theme as well as for How government works and Public service and military. Only Theme Lead institutions can use this template. Mandatory Navigation 2017-01-03
Topic Pages Allows people to discover services and information related to a specific topic across government. Leads people to sub-topics and destination pages. Must be implemented for each topic page on Canada.ca. New topics must be approved for use by the respective Theme Lead. Mandatory Navigation 2017-02-01
Departments and Agencies Page Provides links to all federal government institutions. Gives an overview of how government is organized. Must be implemented only once on Canada.ca. Mandatory Navigation 2017-02-02
Institutional Profile Pages Gives an overview of an institutions mandate, programs, services and information. Must be implemented once for each institution on Canada.ca by institutions listed under the schedules I, I.1, and II of the Financial Administration Act (FAA). Consult the Canada.ca Institutions list. Exceptions may apply* Mandatory Navigation 2015-01-01
Organizational Profile Pages For an organization associated with a government institution. Gives an overview of the organizations mandate, programs, services and information. Must be implemented once for each organization associated with a federal institution on Canada.ca. Consult the Canada.ca Institutions list. Exceptions may apply* Mandatory Navigation 2015-12-31
Partnering/Collaborative Arrangement Profile Pages May give an overview of the mandate and/or the information and services of partnering or collaborative arrangement agreements. Partnering or collaborative arrangements are joint activities or transactions. They have shared or associated objectives, with a written agreement to combine financial or in-kind input. They must involve another government, company, organization, group or individual. Use to introduce each partnering or collaborative arrangement. Consult the Canada.ca Institutions list. Do not use for inter-departmental collaborations within the Government of Canada. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Simple Service Initiation Pages Helps people quickly start a task. Gives people the information they need before starting the task. Gives people easy access to service standards, turnaround times, related services, guides and help.

Use to start tasks that:

  • have a simple set of prerequisites to satisfy prior to starting the task . Prerequisites could be:
    • eligibility criteria;
    • documents or information that must be in hand
  • point to any service delivery channel such as:
    • phone
    • email
    • mail

Don’t use for tasks that do not have any prerequisites. Use the Advanced Service Initiation Page Template instead.

 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Advanced Service Initiation Pages
  • Gives people the information they need before starting the task
  • Explains processes and procedures that are more complex
  • Helps people:
    • quickly initiate a task
    • check eligibility criteria
    • prepare documents
    • engage with third-party representatives
  • Gives easy access to:
    • related services
    • guides and help
    • service standards and average wait times

Use to start tasks that:

  • involve complex processes and multiple steps. For example:
    • applying for a grant
    • funding
    • getting a passport
    • eligibility criteria
    • documents or information that must be in hand
  • point to any service delivery channel such as:
    • phone
    • email
    • mail

Don’t use for tasks that have few or no prerequisites, use the Simple Service Initiation Page Template instead.

 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Institutional Service Standards Pages Collects all service standards from an institution, as per the requirements in the Policy on Service Used only once by each institution that has service standards to make public. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Program Description Page Template Offers detail about a program Use to give background information about a program. Don’t use to start a task. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Ministerial Profile Pages
  • The only source of information about a minister
  • Gives a list of institutions under the minister’s responsibility
  • Gives an overview of the biography and links to the minister’s:
    • recent activities and announcements
    • social media channels

Used only once to give information about:

  • a minister
  • heads of quasi-judicial arm’s-length institutions
  • parliamentary secretaries
 Mandatory Destination 2015-01-01
Collection Landing Pages
  • Allows people to search within a collection
  • Introduces the most popular items from a collection
  • Gives access to an collection of common content produced by all or most federal institutions
  • Gives access to contextual and historical content related to a collection such as reports and events
 Used only once to introduce a collection Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Consultations Landing Page
  • Gives access to Government of Canada’s consultations and engagement activities
  • Helps people find a specific consultation or engagement initiative by filtering results with keywords or metadata fields
  • Displays consultations and engagement activities by topics and institutions
 Use only once to introduce the Government of Canada-wide consultations collection. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Consultations Finder
  • Enables people to efficiently find a specific consultation or engagement activity by quickly filtering results based on:
    • keywords
    • pre-set metadata fields
 Use only once to allow searching and filtering within the Government of Canada-wide consultations collection. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Consultation Profiles
  • Describes of a consultation or engagement activity
  • It can provide:
    • status and outcome of the initiative
    • background materials
    • data
    • information about planned or completed events
 Use for each consultation, to provide an overview of a single consultation or engagement activity. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Act Profile Pages
  • Gives a one or two sentence description written using plain language principles to describe the act
  • Allows people to read the legislation
  • Relates an act to:
    • topics
    • institutions
    • ministers
    • other legislation
  • Gives access to consultations on:
    • the act
    • news
    • notices
    • amendments
 Use to publish an act. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Regulation Profile Pages
  • Gives a one or two sentence description written using plain language principles to describe the regulation
  • Allows people to read the regulation
  • Relates a regulation to:
    • topics
    • institutions
    • ministers
    • other legislation
  • Gives access to consultations on:
    • the regulation
    • news
    • notices
    • amendments
 Use to publish a regulation. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Guidance on Legislation Profile Pages
  • Gives access to guidance on legislation
  • Recaps the guidance using plain language principles
  • Gives access to details and related materials
 Use to publish and explain guidance on legislation. Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Government-Wide Audience Page Template
  • Gives access to information and services relevant to a government-wide audience such as:
    • Indigenous peoples
    • youth
    • veterans
  • Must be implemented only once for each Canada.ca audience.
  • Only used by your departmental lead for the Canada.ca audience.
 Mandatory Navigation 2015-01-01
News Landing Page Template
  • Gives access to Government of Canada’s news products
  • Helps people find a specific news product by filtering results with keywords or metadata fields
  • Displays news products by topics and institutions
 Use only once to introduce the Government of Canada-wide news collection. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
News Results Page
  • Enables people to efficiently find a specific news product by quickly filtering results based on:
    • keywords
    • pre-set metadata fields
 Use only once to allow searching and filtering within the Government of Canada-wide news collection. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
News Product Pages
  • Allows people to read a news product
  • Relates a news product to:
    • topics
    • institutions
    • ministers
  • Must be used to publish the following news products:
    • news release
    • backgrounder
    • speech
    • media advisory
    • statement
 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Promotional Event Pages
  • Describes an official government event that occurs in a promotional context
  • Gives information such as:
    • date
    • time
    • location
    • target audiences
  • May link to content related to the event such as:
    • services
    • programs
  • Use for an event that occurs that is part of a promotional campaign, such as a:
    • festival
    • celebration
    • ceremony
  • Don’t use for non-promotional events that occur as part of regular business activities. Non-promotional events could include:
    • workshops
    • webinars
 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Campaign Pages (reduced header/footer)
  • Provides information in support of a promotional campaign
  • Allows for flexible page design
  • Use to support government-wide advertising or marketing campaigns that last for a set period of time.
  • Use only when specifically requested by the Privy Council Office (PCO).
  • Some exceptions to the above criteria may be permitted, but they must be approved by PCO.
 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Campaign Pages (full header/footer)
  • Gives information to support a promotional campaign/li>
  • Allows for flexible page design
  • Use to support institutional, theme or Government of Canada-wide promotional campaigns that last for a set period of time.
  • Don’t use as a replacement for other page types such as:
    • program delivery
    • service delivery
 Conditional Destination 2015-01-01
Contact Us Pages
  • Provides a consistent model for contact options on Canada.ca
  • Allows people to:
    • quickly find contact information
    • contact the government by email, telephone, in person or by mail
  • Supports multiple ways to find contact information
  • Shows adapted contact information in the context of the web page
  • Use to present contact information for:
    • a person
    • an office
    • an organization
    • an emergency
  • Use to get:
    • feedback
    • complaints
    • questions from people.
 Mandatory Destination 2015-01-01
Search Results Listings Provides the template for the search engine on Canada.ca. Used only once for the Canada.ca search engine. Mandatory Navigation 2015-01-01
Short Index Pages Shows a combined list of select hyperlinked titles that lead to other pages or documents. Use to present a list of 10 to 50 related links without text descriptions. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Long Index Pages Shows a combined list of select hyperlinked titles that lead to other pages or documents. Use to present a list of 50+ related links without descriptions. Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Finder Pages
  • Shows a combined list of select hyperlinked titles that lead to other pages
  • Lets people select only one value per metadata field in order to reduce a large amount of results of items into a shorter list.
  • Metadata fields help people understand how a collection of items is organized.
  • Use to present a list of 100+ related links that can be filtered using metadata and may require results to be split across multiple pages.
  • Use when there is a need to allow people to build simpler queries against large amount of results.
 Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Faceted Finder Pages
  • Presents a combined list of select hyperlinked titles that lead to other pages or documents.
  • Lets people select one or more values per metadata field in order to reduce a large collection of items into a more manageable list.
  • Metadata fields help people understand how a large amount of results is organized
  • Use to present a list of 100+ related links that can be filtered using metadata and may require results to be split across multiple pages.
  • Use when there is a need to allow people to build more complex queries against a large number of results
 Conditional Navigation 2015-01-01
Generic Destination Pages Supports any content that doesn’t have an assigned template.
  • Use to present any content type that does not have an assigned template.
  • Must use existing common design patterns from templates within this specification.
 2015-01-01
