Purpose of this document

The Canada.ca Content and Information Architecture Specification outlines the content and information architecture (IA) approach for Canada.ca through user-centric templates and approaches designed to help users successfully complete tasks.

Evolution of this document

The Content and IA Specification has been iteratively refined on a regular basis since January 2014, and will continue to be refined based upon feedback and user testing.
